Why sponsor

Network with key influencers from across the globe and build strategic relationships with policymakers, public sector leaders and academic researchers who are shaping policy, governance and driving open-source development.

Contribute to the national agenda of innovation positioning Ireland Inc as leaders in emerging tech built on open source and open source AI. Discuss how we address the gaps in skills and collaborative work practices and build the foundation for successful large-scale AI and open source initiatives.

Identify valuable collaboration opportunities with Public Sector, Academia and Enterprises, opening doors for future partnerships and co-innovation. This is especially relevant for enterprises exploring AI and open-source solutions, where public sector collaboration is crucial for scaling innovations that can shape national infrastructure and services.

Address open source and AI concerns, perceived risks around security, intellectual policy, or quality control, while showcasing success stories. Understand how these technologies can safely and effectively be adopted and hear first-hand real-world case studies of successful partnerships that are creating competitive advantages in their sector while mitigating risks.
